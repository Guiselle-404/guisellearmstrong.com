# Content Guidelines - GuiselleArmstrong.com

## Source of Truth
- Professional data should come from `profile-data/` directory when available
- Use exact names, dates, and details from source materials
- When users request creative changes or new content, follow their direction

## Tone and Style Suggestions
- Professional but approachable — this is a job-seeking portfolio
- Action-oriented with strong verbs (Led, Managed, Reduced, Improved)
- Quantified achievements with metrics where possible
- Concise: every line should demonstrate value

## What to Highlight
- Measurable achievements (percentages, numbers, outcomes)
- Career progression and skill growth
- Certifications and professional development

## Content Tips
- Use American English spelling
- Keep sentences readable and scannable
- Bullet points work well for lists of achievements
