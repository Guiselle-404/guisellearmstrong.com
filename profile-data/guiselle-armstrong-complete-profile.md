My background bridges cybersecurity, compliance, and continuous improvement, allowing me to translate technical risk into well-governed, scalable business processes aligned with frameworks such as SOX, SOC 2, PCI DSS, GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, and NIST.

>
> I am passionate about mentoring junior analysts, strengthening risk awareness, and fostering a culture of continuous improvement. With experience spanning GRC engineering, change management, network engineering, and technical support leadership, I thrive in environments where governance, technology, and business objectives intersect. Fluent in English and Spanish, I enjoy working in diverse, fast-paced organizations to deliver secure, compliant, and sustainable solutions that support long-term business goals.
